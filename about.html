Our Story

Finer Palate is a family-owned catering and foodservice management company based in Richmond, VA. Our team has a combined experience of 30 years within the hospitality and food and beverage industry. Our secret blend of professional expertise and family passion culminates into a catering company that offers something truly special.

We cook from the heart and execute every event to the highest standard. Whether we’re serving high-profile guests at an exclusive function or delivering boxed lunches to your office, you’ll experience the difference of a catering company that genuinely cares.

Smart or casual events, large or small, we’ll always make sure to provide a one-of-a-kind experience. It’s not just our food that we consistently craft to the highest standard; it’s the entire atmosphere we create that sets Finer Palate apart.

Our exceptional customer service is where we truly distinguish ourselves. From the moment you get in touch with us, we will dedicate our work to making your event a success. Our goal is to exceed every client’s expectation, and we look forward to creating something truly magical for you.

We want our clients to feel something truly special when they hold an event with us. Not only from the exceptional food but also from the pure sense of enjoyment that each event delivers. With Finer Palate, you’ll never stress about an event again!
